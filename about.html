About

Hey, I'm Bebotian.

I make mods for Minecraft and Geometry Dash, and post the process on YouTube — building things, breaking things, and figuring out how they work along the way.

Right now I'm working on Wildreach, a Minecraft mod built on NeoForge that adds new biomes and structures to explore, and GDmenu, a Geometry Dash mod. Both are still in development — you can follow progress on the Projects page, and everything will land here for download the moment it's ready.

This site is where all of it lives: finished mods, works in progress, and a running log of what's changed. Stick around, or come hang out in the Discord while things get built.
